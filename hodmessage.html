INSTITUTE OF COMPUTER APPLICATION

Head Of Department's Message


Dear Stakeholders,

Institute of Computer Application is committed to achieve excellence in Computing Technology, Research and assesses the success and leadership of its programs using the highest standards of quality, innovative teaching & Learning. Our goal is to establish Center of Excellence in Computer Application to create a platform for academia, industry and R&D to share knowledge. Our aim is to achieve global leadership in human development and excellence in education. Our objectives of imparting professional education, combined with fostering innovative thinking, application of knowledge, inculcating professional ethics and consciousness to social responsibilities, are being met at Institute of Computer Application in an integrated form. Institute of Computer Application plays a vital role in producing world class software developer, required to meet the demands of the fast changing global society. Apart from curriculum based studies, emphasis is laid on promotion of innovative-driven projects, interaction with industries & International Universities, soft skills and personality development, co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. We encourage and promote research activities and integrate it with teaching learning process. We believe in lifelong learning and training, leading to benefits of the society. We at ICA run the Industry linked programs to provide platform for Students and our Industrial partners to work together in the development of computing technology, with excellence in education and research activities, collaborations with global Universities MNCs, we aim to make the institution globally visible. I am confident that with the concerted efforts of ICA, the institute will scale to greater heights of excellence in the years to come.



Institute Of Computer Application At SAGE University Indore
