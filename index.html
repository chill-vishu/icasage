INSTITUTE OF COMPUTER APPLICATION




SAGE Institute of Computer Application


The Department of Computer Application prepares students to design, develop and implement software applications. The academic curriculum helps students develop technical, managerial and soft skills for careers in the IT industry.


Overview

Computer Application is one of the thrust areas in Science and Technology. In appreciation of its growing importance in business and the industrial scenario, the University established an Institute of Computer Application to facilitate research and human resources development in the subject. The Institute offers Master of Computer Application(MCA-2 Years) and Bachelor of Computer Application(BCA-3 Years) with specialization in Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cloud Computing and Cyber Security and research programme leading to Ph.D. Sage Institute of Computer Application is one the Top Best BCA/MCA college in indore, Madhya Pradesh, India.


Vision

To give emphasis and focus on development of competent and skilled computer professionals.


Mission

To provide a student-friendly environment that is amicable for practical knowledge.

To provide an education that transforms students through rigorous coursework and by providing an understanding of the needs of society and industry.

To take continuous efforts to meet the global technological challenges in collaboration with professional from IT industries.


PEO

Be successful professional with exposure to latest technology, knowledge, skills relevant to ICT Industry.

Be able to contribute in synthesizing solutions for real world problem by working in team.

Become global professional, researcher, keen learner, motivated and follower of ethical practices.

Become lifelong learners and imbibe essential skills to serve the society at the highest level
